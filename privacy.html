Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to 256 Travels & Tour (“256 Travels”, “we”, “us”, “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights. By using our website or services, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. What Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you interact with us — e.g., make an enquiry, booking, or contact us — we may collect:

  • Your name, contact details (email, phone, address), and billing details.
  • Travel-related information: passport/visa details, itinerary preferences, travel history, and date of birth.
  • Information needed for bookings (e.g., hotel or transport requirements).
  • Sensitive data provided voluntarily (e.g., special requests, health requirements for medical tourism, or dietary preferences).

1.2 Non-Personal / Technical Information

When you visit our website, we may collect technical data such as:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • IP address and approximate geographic location.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, and interactions (clicks, form submissions).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for purposes including:

  • Processing bookings, payments, cancellations, and refunds.
  • Communicating with you regarding itineraries, visa updates, and travel alerts.
  • Providing customer support and responding to enquiries.
  • Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., sharing required info with immigration authorities).
  • Fraud prevention and ensuring secure transactions.

3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With airlines, hotels, transport operators, and visa agents required to fulfill your booking.
  • Authorities: With government bodies, embassies, and customs where required by law.
  • Third-Party Processors: With payment gateways and IT service providers who assist our operations.

4. Data Security & Storage

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access. Data is stored on secure servers with appropriate access controls. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations). You may also opt-out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your website experience (e.g., remembering preferences, analytics). You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may limit some website functionality.

7. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only as long as required to fulfill booking purposes, legal compliance, and regulatory obligations. After this period, data is anonymized or deleted.
